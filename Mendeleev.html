Dmitri mendeleev

Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev was a Russian chemist and inventor. He is best known for formulating the Periodic Law and creating a farsighted version of the periodic table of elements.

Born: 8 February 1834, Tobolsk, Russia

Died: 2 February 1907, Saint Petersburg, Russia

Awards: Copley Medal, Davy Medal, Demidov Prize

Children: Lyubov Blok, Olga Mendeleeva, Maria Mendeleeva, Vasily Mendeleev, more

Education: Saint Petersburg Imperial University (1865)
